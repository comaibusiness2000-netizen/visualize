Visualize privacy policy.

Last updated: July 22, 2026.

Current TestFlight version

Visualize is currently local-only. Your profile, Why photos, Vision decks, Anti-vision decks, and Self Speech scripts are saved on your device. They are not uploaded to a Visualize server in this version.

Information you add

  • Profile details you type, such as first name, age, and life estimate.
  • Why photos you choose for the people, memories, and future reasons behind your goals.
  • Photos you choose from your photo library for Vision and Anti-vision decks.
  • Self Speech titles and scripts you write.
  • Local app settings such as theme, language, and notification preference.

How your information is used

Your information is used to provide the core app experience: the life clock, Why board, private image decks, presentation playback, and text-to-speech playback of your own scripts.

Photo access

Visualize asks for photo library access only so you can select images for your Why board, Vision deck, and Anti-vision deck. Selected images are copied into the app's local storage on your device.

Audio and speech

Self Speech playback uses the text-to-speech capability available on your device. The current TestFlight version does not upload your scripts to a Visualize server.

Cloud sync and AI

Cloud sync, account creation, image generation, and AI editing are planned future features. They are not active in the current local-only TestFlight version. If they are added later, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before release.

Deleting data

You can reset local data from inside the app. Deleting the app from your device also removes data stored locally by the app.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the support page: Visualize Support.
