Common questions

  • Where is my data saved? The current TestFlight version saves data locally on your device.
  • Will my photos sync to another device? Not in the current local-only version.
  • What happens if I delete the app? Local app data is removed from the device.
  • Does the app upload my Self Speech scripts? No. The current version uses device text-to-speech.
